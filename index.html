Russia

Russia is famous for its vast and varied landscape, which includes towering mountains, dense forests, and wide-open steppes. It is home to a diverse population of over 144 million people, who speak more than 100 languages. Russia has contributed significantly to world culture, with famous authors, composers, artists, and scientists hailing from the country. The country is also known for its iconic landmarks such as Red Square, the Kremlin, and Saint Basil's Cathedral.
