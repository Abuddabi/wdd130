Overview

Purpose

Have a representation of the company in the internet and also give visitors information about company services and other useful information. Attract new customers and create a positive image of the company among the target audience.

Audience

Target customers are people aged 25-45 years, adventure-seekers and outdoor enthusiasts who are interested in whitewater rafting and other outdoor activities. These customers want to experience thrilling outdoor adventures in beautiful natural environments, and may be seeking a break from their daily routines. They likely want to feel challenged, but also want to feel safe and well-guided during their experiences. Their needs may include guidance and information about planning and booking their rafting trip, as well as assurance that they will be well taken care of during their experience. The audience for this website may access it from a variety of devices, including desktops, laptops, and mobile devices, depending on their location and level of convenience.
